Tribute to Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein (1879-1955) was a German-born theoretical physicist who revolutionized our understanding of the universe. His theory of relativity, which introduced the famous equation E=mcÂ², is considered one of the pillars of modern physics.

Einstein received the 1921 Nobel Prize in Physics for his explanation of the photoelectric effect, which was pivotal in establishing quantum theory. Beyond his scientific achievements, Einstein was an outspoken advocate for peace and civil rights.
